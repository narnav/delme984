Lions

Lions are large carnivores and are known as the "king of the jungle". They live in groups called prides.

Sharks

Sharks are predatory fish found in oceans around the world. They have been around for more than 400 million years.

Dogs

Dogs are domesticated mammals that have been bred for thousands of years for various purposes including companionship and work.

Cats

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their independence and agility.
